BIOLOGY AND MEDICINE

From micro-organisms to the human body, biology breakthroughs unveil the wonders of the tiny world. Thanks to advancements in medicine, many enjoy extended life expectancy and improved quality of life. Discover the history of medicine, including milestones like penicillin's discovery and the first heart transplant, often inspired by observations of animals in the wild.

COSMOLOGY

Embark on a cosmic journey with our captivating exhibition, 'The Sky Above Us,' unveiling the mysteries of the night sky. Discover constellations, galaxies, and the celestial wonders that have guided explorers through the ages. Trace the evolution of solar exploration, from ancient Arab traders to Galileo's groundbreaking telescope, to modern-day missions exploring the planets of our solar system.

ECOLOGY

With growing awareness of humanity's impact on Earth, it's increasingly vital for scientists to delve into sustainable solutions for the entire planet's well-being. Our focus extends beyond human needs, striving to safeguard the delicate balance of our global ecosystem for generations to come.
