Want to celebrate your birthday with us?

We are thrilled to see that you are interested in celebrating your special day at our museum! Keep reading to learn about our exciting alternatives.

Birthdays with us are both fun and educational, where you can enjoy celebrating with friends while experiencing something new.

Practical information

Price: 250,- per child (min. price is 2500,-)

Days: Fridays 17:00 - 19:00 and weekends 11:00 - 13:00

Duration: Two (2) hours

Important

There must be 2-4 parents present, and they must be available for the children for the full duration of the party.

Activity options

  • Crystallize your own rock candy
  • Make neon flowers
  • Elephant toothpaste
  • Coffee ground fossils
  • Ice cream in a bag
  • Engineer a drinking straw roller coaster
  • Make a lava lamp
  • Extract DNA from strawberries

Order food

You can choose between ordering food from our cafeteria, or from Peppes Pizza. We have a partnership with the latter, which grants a birthday discount.

It's up to you whether you would like to start or end the party with good. If you choose to order from the cafeteria, we require orders being placed three (3) days in advance.
